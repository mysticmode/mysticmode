Time goes by and does life grow in you?
2022-07-09
Life seems to be good if I try to do and experience things that are versatile. I’m not a specialist in anything and my head wanders around several interests that I’d like to do at the time and I do that.

But when time goes by and age grows, I feel that my body and mind is not feasible enough to do these. It’s interesting to think about that when time goes by, your mental and physical health changes and then you get to do only limited set of things.

And that could lead us to not keep a broader mindset. Just a passing thought from me for today.
