Is it really smart work?
2022-05-02

// no link

<p>Just went to a tea shop, the FM Radio played about doing smart work instead 
of hard work.</p>

<p>And I heard this statement multiple times. I think that is not a valid statement 
to me unless I'm missing something.</p>

<p>Every smartness that comes in you is the hours and days you put your effort in 
to mastery.</p>

<p>IOW, smart work is the asset of hard work.</p>

<p>To put in another way, It is through the hardship, the time gives you the gift 
of experience which is the foundation for smart work.</p>
